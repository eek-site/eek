Enforcement Scope

  • Supplier Enforcement: Suppliers in breach of commercial obligations, pricing terms, communication protocols, or service delivery conditions will be subject to financial remedy, account termination, blacklisting, and jurisdictional enforcement.
  • User Compliance: Users who reverse payments, abandon jobs, misuse services, or fail to comply with service terms may face formal escalation, account revocation, debt recovery, and legal proceedings without further notice.
  • Contract Violations: Any breach of terms, misrepresentation, fraudulent activity, or non-compliance with agreed service conditions will result in immediate enforcement action through appropriate legal channels.
  • Payment Disputes: Unauthorized chargebacks, payment reversals, or attempts to avoid legitimate service charges will be pursued through debt collection agencies and legal proceedings as applicable.
  • Service Abuse: Misuse of emergency services, false claims, harassment of technicians, or abuse of the Eek Mechanical platform will result in permanent service prohibition and potential legal action.
