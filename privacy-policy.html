Privacy Statement

Effective Date: 1 April 2024 · Updated May 2026 (NZ IPP 3A)

Eek Mechanical ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, payment information
  • Usage Information: IP address, device information, browsing history
  • Service-Specific Information: Vehicle and rescue details

2. Indirect collection — Information Privacy Principle 3A (IPP 3A)

Where we collect personal information about you from someone other than you — for example a referrer, insurer, roadside assistance provider, telephony provider, or public motor vehicle register — we comply with Information Privacy Principle 3A of the Privacy Act 2020 (New Zealand). We take steps that are reasonable in the circumstances to ensure you are notified as soon as reasonably practicable after that indirect collection (unless an exception in the Act applies), including:

  • that the information has been collected;
  • the purpose of collection;
  • the intended recipients;
  • the name and address of the agency that collected the information and the agency holding it;
  • if collection is authorised or required by law, which particular law applies;
  • your rights of access to, and correction of, personal information under the information privacy principles.

Guidance: Office of the Privacy Commissioner — Principle 3A.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Services: Fulfill requests and manage inquiries
  • Communication: Respond to inquiries and provide support
  • Marketing: With consent, send promotional materials
  • Improvement: Improve services and customer experience

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors and IT service providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

5. Security

We use appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

6. Your Rights

You may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data.

7. Retention
