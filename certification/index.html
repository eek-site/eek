What is NZIFDA Certification?

The New Zealand Insoluble Fuel Disposal Agency (NZIFDA) is an independent industry authority that establishes and maintains professional standards in the fuel contamination remediation sector. The NZIFDA Mark is the official certification symbol that identifies compliant operators authorized to provide fuel contamination remediation services in New Zealand.

When you see the NZIFDA Mark, you can be confident that the operator:

  • Has completed a comprehensive certification process
  • Meets all regulatory requirements for fuel handling
  • Has properly trained and qualified staff
  • Uses equipment that meets NZIFDA standards
  • Maintains current insurance and licensing
  • Complies with all New Zealand regulations
  • Is committed to quality service and customer protection
