Supplier Agreement

Effective Date: 1 April 2021

1. Definitions

1.1 Company: Eek Mechanical Ltd, including all subsidiaries and affiliates.

1.2 Supplier: The entity providing goods and/or services under this Agreement.

1.3 Goods: Products or materials supplied by the Supplier.

1.4 Services: Work or performance rendered by the Supplier.

1.5 Agreement: This document, including all schedules and policies.

1.6 Applicable Laws: All relevant laws and regulations.

1.7 Force Majeure: Unforeseen events beyond reasonable control.

1.8 IP Rights: Intellectual property rights including copyrights, patents, and trademarks.

2. Becoming an Eek Mechanical Supplier

2.1 Pathways to Supplier Status: A Supplier becomes a participating member by:

  • Completing the supplier registration form at https://www.eek.nz/supplier; or
  • Accepting any job or work order issued by Eek Mechanical.

2.2 Acceptance of Terms: By taking either action above, the Supplier confirms acceptance of all terms.

2.3 Network Participation: Suppliers acknowledge they are part of the Eek Mechanical network. Eek Mechanical may publicly represent the Supplier's participation in customer communications.

2.4 Privacy Waiver: The Supplier grants Eek Mechanical standing waiver to disclose necessary business information to customers.

2.5 Voluntary Withdrawal: To exit the network, submit a formal request via https://www.eek.nz/supplier-relations

2.6 Documentation Requirements: Suppliers must provide all required certifications, insurance, and compliance evidence.

2.7 Payment Contingency: No payment shall be processed unless the Supplier has completed registration and provided all required documentation.

3. Supplier Obligations

3.1 Compliance: Supplier must comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

3.2 Quality Assurance: All Goods and Services must meet or exceed quality standards.

3.3 Ethical Conduct: Supplier must conduct business ethically and avoid conflicts of interest.

3.4 Indemnity and Liability: All liability arising from Goods and Services falls solely on the Supplier. Supplier agrees to indemnify and hold the Company harmless.

3.5 Delivery and Performance: Supplier must deliver Goods and perform Services in a timely and professional manner.

4. Delivery of Goods

4.1 Delivery Requirements: Goods must be delivered to specified location by agreed date with all required documentation.

4.2 Risk and Title: Risk remains with Supplier until successful delivery. Title passes upon full payment.

4.3 Inspection: Company reserves right to inspect Goods and may reject non-compliant items at Supplier's expense.

4.4 No Partial Delivery: Partial deliveries not permitted unless authorized in writing.

4.5 Late Delivery: Supplier is liable for losses from late delivery unless due to Force Majeure.

5. Supply of Services

5.1 Performance Standards: Services shall be provided with due care, skill, and diligence by qualified personnel.

5.2 Timeliness: Services must be performed according to specified timelines.

5.3 Personnel Requirements: All personnel must be properly trained and qualified.

5.4 Corrective Actions: If Services don't meet standards, Supplier must re-perform at own expense.

6. Warranties

6.1 Supplier Warranties: Supplier warrants that:

  • All Goods are free from defects in design, materials, and workmanship
  • All Goods and Services conform to specifications
  • Goods are new, unused, and fit for purpose
  • Services performed with due skill and care

6.2 Legal Compliance: All Goods and Services comply with applicable laws.

6.3 Warranty Period: Minimum 12 months from acceptance date.

6.4 Defects and Remedies: Supplier must repair/replace defective Goods or re-perform Services at own expense.

6.5 Customer Disputes: Supplier accepts full responsibility for resolving customer disputes and shall indemnify Company from customer claims.

7. Payment Terms

7.1 Invoice Requirements: Invoices must reference Purchase Order and provide clear details.

7.2 Disputed Invoices: May be withheld until resolution.

7.3 Reasonable Accounts: If amount charged exceeds amount paid by customer, Eek Mechanical will pay up to total collected from customer less $150.

7.4 Unpaid Customer Debts: Remaining unpaid debts referred back to Supplier for collection.

7.5 Setoff Rights: Company may set off amounts owed against amounts Supplier owes.

7.6 Payment Authority: Full authority over payment timing and terms is ceded to Eek Mechanical.

8. Materials

8.1 Ownership: Materials provided by Eek Mechanical remain Company property.

8.2 Permitted Use: Only for agreed purpose of fulfilling Agreement obligations.

8.3 Return: Must promptly return all materials upon request or termination.

9. Confidentiality

9.1 Confidentiality Obligations: Supplier agrees to keep all Company information confidential.

9.2 Restriction on Financial Disclosures: Supplier shall not disclose financial terms or inquire with customers about amounts paid to Eek Mechanical.

9.3 Breach Penalty: Any confidentiality breach results in forfeiture of outstanding amounts and potential legal action.

10. Intellectual Property

10.1 Ownership: IP rights created under this Agreement become exclusive property of Eek Mechanical.

10.2 Pre-existing IP: Supplier grants non-exclusive, royalty-free, perpetual license for pre-existing IP.

10.3 Third-party IP: Supplier warrants no infringement and shall indemnify Company against claims.

11. Non-Competition

11.1 Non-Competition Obligation: During term and for 2 years after, Supplier shall not compete with Eek Mechanical in same markets.

11.2 Prohibition on Solicitation: Supplier shall not solicit Company's customers.

11.3 Non-Circumvention: Supplier shall not bypass Eek Mechanical to provide services directly to customers.

12. Privacy Compliance (NZ Privacy Act 2020)

12.1 Compliance: Supplier shall comply with New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 and 13 Information Privacy Principles.

12.2 Collection: Collect personal information only for lawful purposes related to this Agreement.

12.3 Storage and Security: Take reasonable steps to protect personal information.

12.4 Privacy Breaches: Must notify Company immediately of any breach.

13. Data Protection

13.1 Compliance: Comply with all applicable data protection laws.

13.2 Use of Data: Use data solely for fulfilling Agreement obligations.

13.3 Prohibited Activities: No marketing, profiling, or unauthorized third-party sharing.

13.4 Data Breach Notification: Notify Company within 24 hours of any breach.

13.5 Retention: Retain data only as required, securely delete upon termination.

14. Relationship Management

14.1 Performance Reviews: Regular meetings to review Supplier performance.

14.2 Reporting: Provide requested information including delivery schedules and operational metrics.

14.3 Issue Resolution: Promptly address performance issues raised by Company.

15. Compliance with Laws

15.1 Legal Compliance: Comply with all laws including anti-bribery, health and safety, employment, and tax laws.

15.2 Company Policies: Comply with all communicated Eek Mechanical policies.

15.3 Modern Slavery: Ensure operations free from forced labour, child labour, and human trafficking.

15.4 Audit Rights: Company may audit Supplier operations for compliance.

16. Jurisdiction and Dispute Resolution

16.1 Jurisdiction: Agreement governed by laws of Northern Ireland. Disputes resolved exclusively in Northern Ireland courts.

16.2 Address for Service:

16.3 Dispute Process: Negotiation, then mediation, then arbitration, then litigation.

16.4 Continued Performance: Supplier must continue fulfilling obligations pending dispute resolution.

17. Audit and Record Retention

17.1 Record-Keeping: Maintain accurate records related to Agreement performance.

17.2 Retention Period: Minimum 6 years following termination.

17.3 Audit Rights: Company may audit records and operations upon reasonable notice.

17.4 Cooperation: Supplier shall fully cooperate during audits.

18. Termination

18.1 Termination for Cause: Company may terminate immediately for material breach, insolvency, or misconduct.

18.2 Termination for Convenience: Company may terminate with 30 days' written notice.

18.3 Supplier's Right: Supplier may terminate if Company fails to make undisputed payments within 30 days of notice.

18.4 Consequences: Supplier must cease using Company materials and return all confidential information.

18.5 Survival: Confidentiality, indemnity, IP, audit, and dispute resolution clauses survive termination.

19. General Provisions

19.1 Entire Agreement: This Agreement supersedes all prior agreements including credit applications and personal guarantees, which are hereby cancelled and nullified.

19.2 Precedence: This Agreement takes precedence over conflicting documents.

19.3 Right to Amend: Eek Mechanical may amend terms at any time without notice. Continued supply constitutes acceptance.

19.4 Assignment: Supplier cannot assign rights without consent. Company may assign to affiliates.

19.5 Independent Contractors: Parties are independent contractors, not partners or agents.

20. Miscellaneous

20.1 Governing Law: Laws of Northern Ireland apply.

20.2 Third-Party Rights: No third-party enforcement rights under Contracts (Rights of Third Parties) Act 1999.

20.3 No Partnership: No partnership, joint venture, or agency relationship created.

20.4 Counterparts: Agreement may be executed in multiple counterparts.
