What Insurance Typically Covers

Professional Fuel Extraction Service:

  • Complete fuel removal from tank and fuel system
  • Fuel system cleaning and flushing
  • Fresh fuel replacement
  • System testing and verification
  • Service warranty

Important Note: Insurance typically covers fuel extraction to PREVENT damage. If you start the engine and cause damage, insurance may cover extraction but may NOT cover the engine damage repair costs. Prevention (extraction) is always better than repair.
