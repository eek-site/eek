Signs You Need Fuel Extraction

1. Wrong Fuel Type Added

  • You put petrol in a diesel vehicle
  • You put diesel in a petrol vehicle
  • You're unsure which fuel type your vehicle uses
  • You used the wrong fuel pump nozzle

2. Engine Not Started Yet

  • You realize the mistake before starting the engine
  • You're still at the petrol station
  • You haven't turned the key or pressed the start button
  • No fuel has circulated through the system

3. Engine Started But Not Driven

  • You started the engine but immediately realized the mistake
  • Engine may have stalled or run roughly
  • You turned off the engine immediately
  • Limited fuel circulation has occurred

4. Engine Started and Vehicle Driven

  • You drove the vehicle after putting wrong fuel in
  • Engine is running poorly or has stalled
  • Warning lights have appeared
  • Extensive fuel circulation has occurred

What to Do When You Need Fuel Extraction

Step 1: STOP - Don't Start the Engine

If you haven't started the engine yet, DO NOT start it. This is critical - starting the engine circulates contaminated fuel through your entire fuel system.

Step 2: Don't Drive the Vehicle

Even if the engine starts, don't drive. Driving circulates contaminated fuel extensively and increases damage risk.

Step 3: Call Eek Mechanical Immediately

Call us immediately for professional fuel extraction. We provide priority dispatch for wrong fuel emergencies across all of New Zealand.

Step 4: Provide Details

When you call, tell us: how much wrong fuel was added, whether the engine was started, your vehicle make/model/year, and your location.
