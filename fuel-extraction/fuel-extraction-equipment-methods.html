Fuel Extraction Methods

Method 1: Direct Tank Drainage

  • Access fuel tank through access point or removal
  • Use extraction pump to remove all contaminated fuel
  • Complete drainage ensures no contaminated fuel remains
  • Most effective when engine hasn't been started

Method 2: Fuel System Flushing

  • Flush fuel lines to remove contaminated fuel
  • Purge fuel pump and delivery system
  • Clean fuel injectors when necessary
  • Required when fuel has circulated through system

Method 3: Complete System Service

  • Comprehensive extraction and cleaning
  • Filter replacement
  • System component inspection
  • Full system testing and verification
